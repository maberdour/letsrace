# LetsRace.cc Privacy Overview

## What we collect
- Email addresses and mailing preferences for the weekly digest.
- Basic analytics (aggregated) to understand site usage.
- Event submission details provided by clubs or organisers.

## How we use it
- To send the weekly email the subscriber requested.
- To keep LetsRace.cc running safely and improve the listings experience.
- To respond to support questions or feedback.

## What we do not do
- No ad tracking pixels.
- No data brokerage or sharing with third parties, unless required by law.
- No marketing without explicit consent.

## Your controls
- Unsubscribe from emails at any time via the link in every message.
- Request a copy of your data or ask for deletion by emailing hello@letsrace.cc.
- Cookies are limited to strictly necessary features; analytics use privacy-friendly tools only.
